Introduction

Welcome to INVENTEX, the next-generation inventory management solution. We've reimagined how businesses track, manage, and analyze their stock, moving away from clunky spreadsheets to a sleek, native Windows experience.

System Requirements

To ensure the smoothest experience, your system should meet the following requirements:

OS: Windows 10 (Version 2004 or later) or Windows 11 Architecture: x64 or ARM64 RAM: 4GB Minimum (8GB Recommended) Storage: 500MB available space

First-time Setup

After downloading the installer, run the executable. INVENTEX handles all dependency installations automatically. Once installed, log in with your credentials to start syncing your workspace instantly.

Why Local-First?

We believe in speed. INVENTEX stores your data locally for zero-latency operations and syncs to the cloud in the background. This ensures you can keep working even when your internet connection drops.
