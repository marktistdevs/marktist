How We Work

We're not your typical agency. No office politics, no micromanagement, no BS. Just real people doing work they actually care about — on their own terms.

01

Our Pillars

100% Remote

Work from literally anywhere

Your apartment in Algiers, a café in Paris, a cabin in the mountains — we don't care where you are. No surveillance software, no mandatory webcams, no check-ins every 5 minutes. Trust is our default. As long as the work gets done well, you're golden. We believe great work doesn't need a physical office — it needs focus, autonomy, and respect.

Flat Structure

Every voice matters equally

No corporate hierarchy, no gatekeepers, no "let me check with my manager." Every team member has a seat at the table for decisions that matter. Your ideas carry weight regardless of when you joined or what your title says. We make decisions together — openly, honestly, and with everyone's input valued.

Freelance Model

We call when we need you

We're not going to spam you when there's nothing to do. We reach out when there's actual work — real projects, real deadlines, real impact. No busywork, no invented tasks, no "looking busy" culture. When there's work, we'll be in touch. When there isn't, enjoy your freedom.

Fully Flexible

Your time is 100% yours

Between projects? You're free to work with whoever you want. We don't do exclusivity clauses, jealousy, or guilt trips. Build your portfolio, take other gigs, grow your skills elsewhere, start a side project. We'll be here when the next project drops — no questions asked.

02

What We Believe

Trust by Default

We hire adults and treat them like adults. No approval chains for basic decisions, no tracking software, no micromanagement. We assume you know what you're doing — and if you don't, we help you figure it out.

Radical Honesty

We tell you what's working and what's not — directly, kindly, and quickly. You do the same. No politics, no passive-aggressive messages, no surprises in reviews. Just real conversations between real people.

Quality Over Speed

We don't do "move fast and break things." We prefer "move thoughtfully and build things that last." Rushing leads to rework. We'd rather take the time to do it right the first time.

People First

Your mental health matters more than any deadline. Burnout isn't a badge of honor — it's a failure of planning. We build sustainable rhythms, respect time off, and understand that life happens.

"We built the team we always wanted to be part of — transparent, respectful, and actually enjoyable to work with."

03

What That Means For You

No pointless meetings

We're async-first. Meetings are rare, always have an agenda, and are optional by default. We respect that deep work requires uninterrupted focus. Your calendar stays yours.

Work when you're at your best

Early bird? Night owl? Chaotic middle-of-the-day bursts? We don't police your schedule. Different time zones, different rhythms — as long as communication flows and deadlines are met.

Fair pay, on time, every time

You do the work, you get paid — simple as that. No chasing invoices, no "we'll pay you next month," no awkward conversations. We pay fairly, we pay quickly, and we pay reliably.

Projects you can be proud of

We take on work that matters. You won't be designing another forgettable banner — you'll be building things people actually use, remember, and love. Work that belongs in your portfolio.

Room to grow

Want to learn something new? Explore a different discipline? We encourage experimentation. Resources, mentorship, and genuine support — whatever helps you become better at what you love.

Clear communication

No guessing games. You'll always know what we expect, when we expect it, and who to ask if something's unclear. We over-communicate context so you can do your best work.

How Projects Work

01

We reach out

When there's a project that fits your skills, we'll message you with the details — scope, timeline, budget. No pressure, no obligations.

02

You decide

If it works for you, you're in. If not, no hard feelings — we'll catch you on the next one. Your availability is always respected.

03

We collaborate

Brief, check-ins as needed, feedback loops. Simple tools, clear communication. You focus on the craft, we handle the logistics.

04

We deliver & pay

Project wraps, payment goes out promptly. Then you're free until the next project — or forever, if that's what you want.
